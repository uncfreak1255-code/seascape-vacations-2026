🤖 GEO Strategy — Generative Engine Optimization

This is your secret weapon. Most of your competitors have zero GEO optimization. By getting this right, you'll show up when travelers ask ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Google AI Overviews: "Where should I stay in Bradenton FL?"

Why GEO Matters for Vacation Rentals

Gartner predicts traditional search volume will drop 25% as users shift to AI-powered search. Google AI Overviews now reach 2B+ monthly users. ChatGPT serves 800M users weekly. When someone asks "best vacation rentals near Anna Maria Island" — you want to be the answer AI cites.
