Planning the Perfect Family Vacation to Anna Maria Island

Updated January 2025 • 12 min read

Anna Maria Island is one of Florida's best family destinations. Here's everything you need to know for an unforgettable trip with kids.

Best Beaches for Families

Manatee Beach is the top choice—lifeguards, restrooms, playground nearby, and gentle waves perfect for little ones.

Kid-Friendly Activities

  • Dolphin watching tours
  • Kayaking through mangroves
  • Bike rentals on the island
  • Fishing from the piers
  • Two Scoops ice cream shop
