Types of Rental Companies on Anna Maria Island

AMI vacation rentals generally fall into three categories:

1. Boutique Local Managers (Like Seascape Vacations)

Smaller companies managing 5-25 properties with hands-on, personalized service. You'll typically get direct communication with the owner or manager, better-maintained properties, and more flexibility. These companies often offer the best direct-booking savings since they have lower overhead.

üí° Why Boutique Matters

With fewer properties to manage, boutique companies invest more time and attention into each home. Guests notice the difference in cleanliness, maintenance, and personalized touches like welcome baskets and local recommendations.

2. Large Regional Property Managers

Companies managing 50-200+ properties across the island and surrounding areas. They have more inventory and availability but may offer less personalized service. Response times can be slower, and properties may vary more in quality since they're harder to maintain consistently at scale.

3. Individual Owner Listings (Airbnb/VRBO)

Property owners who self-manage through platforms like Airbnb and VRBO. Quality is highly inconsistent ‚Äî some owners are meticulous, others are rarely on-site. You'll pay platform service fees (typically 12-18% on Airbnb) and may have difficulty reaching someone for emergencies.

Questions to Ask Before Booking

Send these questions to any rental company before committing:

  1. What is the total all-in cost? Including cleaning fee, taxes, and any additional fees
  2. What is your cancellation policy? Especially for weather-related cancellations
  3. Do you have local staff? Or is support handled remotely?
  4. When was the property last updated? Ask about renovations and maintenance
  5. What's the check-in/check-out process? Self-check-in vs. meeting someone
  6. Are linens and towels provided? Some companies charge extra
  7. Is there a pool heater? Essential for winter stays
  8. How close is the nearest beach access? Verify with a map, not just the listing
