Best Time to Visit Anna Maria Island: A Month-by-Month Guide

Updated January 2025 • 9 min read

Peak Season (December – April)

Perfect weather, minimal rain, but higher prices and crowds. Book 3-6 months ahead.

Shoulder Season (May & November)

Our favorite time! Great weather, fewer crowds, better rates. The sweet spot.

Summer (June – August)

Hot and humid with afternoon storms, but lowest prices and uncrowded beaches.

Fall (September – October)

Hurricane season risk but incredible deals. Water is warmest for swimming.
