15 Best Restaurants Near Anna Maria Island

Updated January 2025 • 10 min read

From fresh Gulf seafood to waterfront dining, here are our top restaurant picks—the places where locals actually eat. If you're planning a visit, check out our complete Anna Maria Island vacation guide for more local tips.

Waterfront Dining

1. The Sandbar Restaurant

Literally on the beach with your toes in the sand. Their grouper sandwich is legendary, and the sunset views are unbeatable.

2. Mar Vista Dockside

A true Old Florida experience in a 1912 building. Arrive by boat or car for fresh seafood on the dock.

3. Dry Dock Waterfront Grill

Upscale-casual with incredible seafood towers and craft cocktails.

Casual Beach Eats

4. Skinny's Place

An island institution since 1989. No-frills burgers that are absolutely legendary.

5. Rod & Reel Pier

Dine at the end of the pier with 360-degree water views. Simple, fresh, iconic.
