An honest, category-by-category comparison from locals who know both cities inside out.

Day Trips from Each City

Both Bradenton and Sarasota are perfectly positioned for day trips along the Gulf Coast. Here's where to go from each:

From Bradenton

  • Anna Maria Island: 15 minutes. The island's small-town beach charm is Bradenton's biggest draw ‚Äî explore our AMI guide
  • Robinson Preserve: 10 minutes. 682 acres of mangrove trails, kayak launches, and observation towers. Free admission and one of the best nature experiences in the region
  • Tampa: 45 minutes north. Busch Gardens, the Florida Aquarium, and Ybor City's nightlife
  • Emerson Point Preserve: 20 minutes. Hiking, kayaking, ancient Native American mounds, and stunning bay views at the tip of the Snead Island peninsula

From Sarasota

  • Siesta Key: 15 minutes. The #1-ranked beach in America (according to Dr. Beach) with the finest, whitest quartz sand you've ever felt ‚Äî full Siesta Key guide
  • St. Armands Circle: 10 minutes. Upscale shopping and dining on a beautiful circular plaza on the way to Lido Key
  • Myakka River State Park: 30 minutes. One of Florida's oldest and largest state parks. Airboat tours, gator spotting, and the canopy walkway are highlights
  • Venice: 30 minutes south. Famous for shark tooth hunting on its beaches ‚Äî a unique Florida experience
