Where to See Dolphins and Manatees Near Bradenton

Updated January 2025 • 7 min read

One of the magical things about staying in our waterfront properties? You can often see dolphins and manatees right from your backyard!

Best Spots for Dolphin Watching

  • Anna Maria City Pier – Free viewing, especially at sunrise
  • Rod & Reel Pier – Dolphins often feed near fishermen
  • Our waterfront rentals – Daily sightings from the dock!

Manatee Season

Winter months (November-March) are best for manatees as they seek warmer waters. The Manatee Viewing Center in Apollo Beach is a guaranteed sighting spot.
