Why Booking Direct Saves You Hundreds on Vacation Rentals

Updated January 2025 • 6 min read

If you've ever booked through Airbnb, VRBO, or Booking.com, you've noticed those service fees at checkout. On a $3,000 week-long rental, these fees can add $300-500 to your total cost. There's a better way.

The Hidden Cost of OTAs

When you book through platforms like Airbnb or VRBO, you're paying:

  • Guest service fees: 5-15% of your booking
  • Host fees: 3-5% (often passed to you)
  • Inflated prices: Hosts raise rates to cover fees

5 Reasons to Book Direct

1. Save 10-20% Instantly

No middleman, no markup. Our direct prices are always the same or lower than OTAs.

2. Better Communication

Direct line to the people who actually manage the property. Get answers in minutes.

3. More Flexibility

Need to change dates or add a night? We can accommodate requests that rigid OTA systems can't.

4. Local Expertise

We live here. Get insider tips on restaurants, beaches, and hidden gems.

5. Build a Relationship

Returning guests get priority booking, special discounts, and first access to new properties.
