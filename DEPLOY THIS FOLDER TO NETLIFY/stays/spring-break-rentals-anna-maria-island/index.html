Spring Break Rentals Near Anna Maria Island

Spring break on AMI is a family affair — this isn't Cancún. The island's strict building codes mean no high-rises, no megaclubs, and no spring break chaos. Instead, you get uncrowded beaches, free trolley rides, and an island pace that families and couples love.

March and April offer ideal Gulf Coast weather: highs in the low 80s, Gulf water warming to the mid-70s, and minimal rain. Spring break is peak season, so book early. Our properties are in Bradenton, giving you easy AMI access without island-level pricing.
