Why Guests Love Anna Maria Island

Anna Maria Island is a 7-mile barrier island on Florida's Gulf Coast, known for its relaxed atmosphere, stunning white-sand beaches, and authentic Old Florida character. Unlike more developed coastal areas, AMI has preserved its small-town charm with local boutiques, waterfront restaurants, and no high-rise buildings.

The island is divided into three distinct communities: Anna Maria City at the north end (historic, quiet), Holmes Beach in the middle (family-friendly, most amenities), and Bradenton Beach at the south (affordable, beach bars). Each offers a unique vacation experience.

With warm Gulf waters, incredible fishing, and some of the most beautiful sunsets in Florida, Anna Maria Island consistently ranks among the state's top beach destinations.
